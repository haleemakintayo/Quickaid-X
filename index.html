Departments

At QuickAid, we connect you with a comprehensive network of specialized departments, ensuring you receive the best possible care for your specific needs. Below are the primary departments available through our app:.

  • Cardiology
  • Emergency Medicine
  • Hepatology
  • Pediatrics
  • Eye Care

Cardiology

Focused on the diagnosis and treatment of heart conditions and diseases, our cardiology department provides expert care for heart attacks, arrhythmias, and other cardiovascular issues. Our cardiologists are equipped with the latest technology to offer comprehensive cardiac care.

Emergency Medicine

Available 24/7, our emergency medicine department provides immediate care for urgent medical conditions. Our emergency physicians and nurses are trained to handle a wide range of emergencies, ensuring you receive timely and effective treatment.

Hepatology

Specializing in liver, gallbladder, biliary tree, and pancreas disorders, our hepatology department offers expert care for conditions such as hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. Our hepatologists are dedicated to providing personalized treatment plans.

Pediatrics

Our pediatrics department offers comprehensive healthcare services for infants, children, and adolescents. From routine check-ups to treatment of complex conditions, our pediatricians ensure the health and well-being of your child.

Eye Care

The eye care department provides a wide range of services, including vision testing, treatment of eye diseases, and surgical procedures. Our ophthalmologists and optometrists are committed to preserving and improving your vision.

Frequently Asked Questions

QuickAid is an emergency response app that helps you find nearby hospitals and medical facilities quickly. It also allows you to register emergency contacts and send alerts in case of an emergency.

  • How does QuickAid work?

    QuickAid uses real-time location data to identify the nearest healthcare providers. It also enables you to send emergency alerts to your pre-registered contacts with just a single tap.

  • What features does QuickAid offer?

    Emergency Alerts: Send alerts to pre-registered contacts during emergencies.

    Location Detection: Pinpoints your location using GPS to find nearby medical facilities.

    Health Facility Directory: Provides a list of nearby hospitals and clinics.

    User Medical Profile: Stores vital medical information for quick access.

    Secure Database: Ensures the protection of your personal and medical data

  • Can I customize my emergency contacts?

    Yes, you can add, edit, or remove your emergency contacts in the app’s settings.

  • Is my data secure with QuickAid?

    Absolutely. QuickAid uses high-level security measures to protect your personal and medical information, ensuring it is encrypted and stored securely.

  • Does QuickAid work internationally?

    QuickAid is designed to work globally as long as you have an internet connection and location services enabled on your device.

  • How does QuickAid detect my location?

    QuickAid uses integrated GPS technology to determine your real-time location, helping you find nearby health facilities quickly and accurately.
